ABOUT DRAX

Drax Project are a four piece band from Wellington, New Zealand. They started out doing late night busking sessions in the City - creating their own unique versions of hip-hop and pop tracks. After entertaining crowds of hundreds on the street, they began using what they'd learned, and earned, on writing and recording original music. Their debut EP was well received, ultimately resulting in their signing to Universal. While still known for their killer live remixes - the boys have been working hard to produce studio records that reflect where they want to take their sound.
